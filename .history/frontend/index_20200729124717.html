Services

Local stores online.

E-Commerce

We provide a platform for all the local stores to go online with an effortless mode to upload their products on their fingertips and those products can be sold offline as well.

Digital Marketing

We intend to promote your products and services online by changing the way to get your customer's attention and provide a chat box to know the needs of a customer.

Web Security

Encrypted databases, additional access security for your data and fault tolerant database. Your data is safe with us.

Application Design

  • Retailer Interface

    A web application and an android application for the retailers to upload their product details and images. A customer-request based order fulfilling interface on the web application.

  • Customer Interface

    A mobile application for android and iOS users to search for the product of their need at the nearest retailer and order them. A feature to ask or show a product of their need to the retailers in the nearest region.

  • Delivery Interface

    An android application for the delivery agents to connect to retailers, register, and deliver the product to the customers with inbuilt map navigation to the destination.
